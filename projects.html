About

This is where I store information about projects I have done and am working on. These projects are organized into software and non-software projects. Software projects can relate to work or school, as well as projects I have worked on for fun, with friends or independently. Non-software projects tend to be indepenent work.

I use the word project pretty loosly. The featured projects vary from working software applications to hobbies like reading and chess.

The projects pages aim to communicate big-picture ideas, the details, show-off project highlights, and communicate lessons I learned along the way.

Check out my software projects here, and my non-software projects here. These can also be accessed from this website's menu (icon in top-left).
